Meng Fang

I am an Assistant Professor in AI at University of Liverpool. I'm also a visiting (assistant) professor at Eindhoven University of Technology (TU/e). I co-lead the UTS NLP Group. I had been a research scientist / intern at Tencent Robotics X / AI, CSIRO and Microsoft Research Asia before.

My research goal is to build trustworthy and intelligent agents capable of human-like language understanding, reasoning, and decision-making. My main areas include NLP and RL.

Question & Answering
